Jing Dong
I am a first-year PhD student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. I received my bachelor degree from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in computer science. My research interest is mainly on learning theory, particularly on sequential learning problems (Bandits & RL). I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Baoxiang Wang and Prof. Guang Cheng.
